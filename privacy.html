Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Hulovo AI ("we", "us", or "our") operates an AI-powered TikTok Shop operations platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account registration information (name, email address)
  • TikTok Shop store data synced via TikTok Shop Partner API (products, orders, reviews)
  • Influencer management data you enter manually
  • Usage data and platform interaction logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our platform services
  • To sync and display your TikTok Shop store data
  • To generate AI-powered insights, review analysis, and content recommendations
  • To send service-related notifications
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. TikTok Shop API Data

We access your TikTok Shop data only with your explicit authorization via OAuth. We use this data solely to provide the services you request and do not share it with third parties except as described in this policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • AI service providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) to generate analysis and content — only the minimum necessary data is sent
  • Infrastructure providers for hosting and storage
  • Law enforcement when required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. API keys and access tokens are stored securely and never exposed in logs or responses.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at zhancen333@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.
